About us

ABOUT BELL JUMBO Bell Jumbo heightens tastes in Indian cuisine. It is a 500 cover restaurant situated on the Theni highway in the South Indian city of Madurai; the menu is faithful to tradition, with many South Indian and North Indian classic recipes with occasional twists that brings variation to the dish. The chefs are brought from various parts of India to maintain the consistency and tradition. This is a family restaurant with ample seating and a play area for kids. The restaurant also houses “Puppys Bakery” providing a host of baked delicacies.
